A collection of watches I'm interested in, along with resources for straps and repair.

## The Wishlist

### Seiko

**Seiko SPB317** (Willard referenced)

The Captain Willard reissue. A proper tool watch with that iconic cushion case.

**Seiko SPB313** (white, which looks magnificent)

The white dial version is stunning. Clean, classic, and versatile.

**Seiko SJE093**

An elegant dress watch from Seiko's Presage line.

### Dive Watches

**[Tudor PELAGOS FXD GMT](https://www.tudorwatch.com/en/watches/pelagos-fxd/m2542gxx7nu-0002)**

The fixed strap bars give it that true military aesthetic. Plus you can [build your own](https://www.tudorwatch.com/en/watches/pelagos-fxd/m2542gxx7nu-0002?configurator=m2542gxx7nu-0002) configuration.

**[Marathon 41MM GSAR](https://www.marathonwatch.com/collections/gsar/products/large-divers-automatic-gsar-with-stainless-steel-bracelet-41mm)**

Model: WW194006SS-0112. Proper military spec dive watch. Used by actual military divers.

### Dress & Luxury

**[Santos De Cartier](https://www.cartier.com/en-us/watches/collections/santos-de-cartier/santos-de-cartier-watch-CRWSSA0029.html)**

The original pilot's watch. That square case with exposed screws is iconic.

**[Vacheron Constantin 222](https://www.vacheron-constantin.com/us/en/collections/historiques/4200h-222a-b934.html)** - Historiques

The 222 is a masterclass in integrated bracelet sports watches.

**Oris Big Crown Pointer Date 80th Anniversary Edition**

A beautiful pilot's watch with that unique pointer date complication.

### Microbrands & Independents

**[anOrdain](https://anordain.com/)** (pretty much anything)

Scottish watchmaker known for their incredible enamel dials. Everything they make is gorgeous.

**[Unimatic](https://www.unimaticwatches.com)**

Italian minimalist tool watches. Clean designs, no-nonsense approach.

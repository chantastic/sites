1. Quick hook
1. Overlay chapters (main points)
1. Hard cuts (push/slide) where bringing screens in
1. Get to the point faster
1. Punch in only at the end of a sentance to land a point
1. Just use 3 text styles
  - Full screen text (overlay if you want to stay on that screen)
  - Progress: steps/chapters
  - Split-screen
1. Ask: how do I show this in the edit visibly? (How do I make it physical)

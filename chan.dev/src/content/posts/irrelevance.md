I feel the fear of replaceability.  
Irrelevance.

When I feel that way, I've always looked for comfort a layer below my typical operation.

Not a new tool.  
Not a new job.  
A [deeper understanding](/a-layer-below) of the thing everything else sits on.

It means that I'm more capable than average.  
Understanding is always the unlock.

And yeah, I get the irony that none of my skills are hard survival skills.  
But we're talking about feelings, not facts.

The fear isn't rational.  
The response doesn't have to be either.  
It just has to work.

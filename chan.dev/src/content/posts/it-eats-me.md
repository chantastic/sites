Watches, power tools, keyboard layouts, microphones, PC builds.

When I find a new thing, it eats me.

Not casually.  
Not "oh I picked up a hobby."  
I mean consumed.  
Research phase. Buying phase. Configuring phase.  
Then the plateau where it becomes just another thing in the garage.

I know this about myself.  
I've known it for twenty years.  
And I still can't tell whether the consumption *is* the learning or if it's avoidance dressed up as productivity.

Probably both.

The honest version of "I'm a maker" is: I'm consumed first, maker second.  
The making only happens after the obsession cools to a temperature I can hold.
